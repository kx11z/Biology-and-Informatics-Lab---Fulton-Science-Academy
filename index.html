About FSABBL

At Fulton Science Academy, students have the opportunity to pursue research on-site in a fully equipped biology lab called the Biomedical & Bioinformatics Laboratory, or FSABBL.

Introductory Biology Lab Club

By participating in our Introductory Biology Lab Club, students gain the skills they need to become knowledgeable about general biology research. After building this foundation, they can join specialized groups to explore more advanced research topics.

Independent Research

Students who do not find a specific interest in our offered clubs can still apply to pursue independent research topics. If accepted, the lab will help them carry out their project as effectively as possible.

Charlie is a senior at Fulton Science Academy. His current research includes experimenting with IPSC-derived neuron models in MEA platforms and the behavior of neuron synaptogenesis mechanisms. His main interest is in software automated testing and experimentation to investigate the limits of BoC model capabilities in terms of intelligence and eventually achieving a platform for in vivo brain organoid BoC research.
